# Weekly Marketing Ops Cadence

The marketing org runs on a five-day rhythm. Same beats every week. If you skip a beat, the funnel goes blind for seven days. Don't skip.

## Monday: Metrics Review (90 min)

Pull last week's numbers before standup. Required dashboards:

- CAC by channel (paid social, paid search, organic, referral)
- MQL volume and MQL to SQL conversion
- Pipeline created (sourced + influenced)
- Content velocity (posts shipped vs posts planned)
- Top 3 LP conversion rates

Targets we hold:

- Blended CAC under $180
- MQL to SQL conversion above 22%
- Content velocity at or above 90% of plan

If two consecutive weeks miss target on any metric, open a fix ticket Monday afternoon. No "let's wait and see."

## Tuesday: Creative Brief

Brief the creative pod on next week's drops. One-page brief per asset:

- Audience segment + insight
- Job-to-be-done
- Hook variants (3 minimum)
- Format + length
- Distribution channel
- Success metric

No brief, no shoot. No exceptions.

## Wednesday: Paid Pipeline Check

Sit with media buyer. Walk every active campaign. For each, decide: scale, hold, kill. Rules:

- Scale if CPA is 30% under target for 3 consecutive days
- Hold if within 15% of target
- Kill if CPA is 40% over target for 5 consecutive days, no exceptions

Document the call in the paid-ops doc. Future-you will want the audit trail.

## Thursday: Content Review

Editor reviews everything shipping next week. Every asset gets red-line feedback before publish. Brand voice checklist:

- No banned words
- Founder voice, not corporate
- Numbers cited, sources linked
- CTA aligned with funnel stage

## Friday: Handoff

End-of-week handoff doc to founder + head of sales. Three sections:

- What shipped (with links)
- What's at risk (with owner + due date)
- What I need from you next week

15 minutes max to write. Sales reads it Monday morning. Founder reads it before the weekend.

## The one rule

If a week breaks cadence, the next week starts with a written post-mortem. We don't skip Mondays.
