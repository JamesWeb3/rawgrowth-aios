# Short-Form Video Playbook

Short-form is hook plus payoff. If you don't earn the next 3 seconds, the next 30 don't matter. Optimize for retention, not duration.

## The formula

```
0-3s:   Hook (the promise + the contradiction)
3-8s:   Setup (the context, who this is for)
8-25s:  Payoff (the actual content, 1-3 beats)
25-30s: Close (CTA or pattern interrupt to rewatch)
```

Three to five B-roll cuts in the first 10 seconds. Static talking-head past 5 seconds is a scroll signal.

## Hook patterns that hold

These work because they create a prediction error in the viewer's brain:

- "Stop doing X. Do Y instead."
- "I tested 7 ways to do X. Only one worked."
- "The mistake every founder makes in their first hire."
- "If you're doing X, you're losing $Y a month."
- "Most people get this wrong. Here's the real answer."
- "What I wish I knew before I [milestone]."

The hook should fit on screen as overlay text in 2 lines max, font readable at 50% zoom (Reels gets cropped).

## Captions and overlay

- Captions burned in (auto-captions plus manual cleanup)
- Overlay hook on for the first 3 seconds, then fade
- Sans-serif, white with black stroke, never fancy
- Bottom third: never put text where the platform UI will cover it

## B-roll cuts

Every 2-3 seconds. Cuts I keep on hand:

- Hands typing
- Whiteboard with the framework drawn
- Screen recording of the actual product
- Walking shot (movement = momentum)
- Zoom-in on the eyes (used sparingly, for emphasis)

If you don't have B-roll, cut to text-on-screen with the key phrase. Never just sit on the talking head.

## Posting time windows (Brazil + US East)

| Platform | Best windows (BRT) | Cadence |
|----------|--------------------|---------|
| Reels | 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 8-10 PM | 1-2/day |
| TikTok | 11 AM-1 PM, 7-10 PM | 1-3/day |
| Shorts | 9-11 AM, 5-7 PM | 1/day |
| LinkedIn video | 7-9 AM Tue/Wed/Thu | 2-3/week |

Test your own audience for 2 weeks, then adjust. The general windows are starting points, not laws.

## Comment-bank for fast engagement

The first hour after posting is where the algorithm decides whether to push. Have 10-15 pre-written replies ready:

- "Which of these are you struggling with right now?"
- "What's your take on [specific point in video]?"
- "Saving this for later"
- "Drop a [emoji] if you're going to try this"

Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes. After that, every 2 hours for the first day.

## What kills short-form

- Logo bumper at the start (you have 3 seconds, don't waste them)
- Long intros ("Hey guys, today I want to talk about...")
- Watermarks from another platform (TikTok WM on Reels = downranked)
- Posting and ghosting (no comment engagement = no second push)
- Vertical video cropped to 16:9 (always shoot 9:16 native)
